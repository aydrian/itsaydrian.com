## The Humanizer Rules (summarized)

Watch for these AI writing patterns:
1. **Inflated symbolism** — "stands as a testament", "serves as", "marks a pivotal moment"
2. **Promotional language** — "boasts", "vibrant", "rich", "renowned", "must-visit", "nestled"
3. **Superficial -ing analyses** — "highlighting...", "underscoring...", "symbolizing...", "reflecting...", "showcasing..."
4. **Vague attributions** — "Industry reports", "Experts argue", "Some critics say"
5. **Em dash overuse** — multiple em dashes per sentence/paragraph
6. **Rule of three** — forcing ideas into groups of three
7. **AI vocabulary** — "additionally", "delve", "fostering", "landscape" (abstract), "testament", "underscore", "tapestry"
8. **Negative parallelisms** — "Not only...but also...", "It's not just about..., it's..."
9. **Generic positive conclusions** — "The future looks bright", vague upbeat endings
10. **Filler phrases** — "In order to", "Due to the fact that", "It is important to note"
11. **Copula avoidance** — "serves as" instead of "is", "boasts" instead of "has"
12. **Elegant variation** — synonym cycling to avoid repetition
13. **Sycophantic tone** — "Great question!", "You're absolutely right!"
